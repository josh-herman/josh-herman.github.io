        Western New Yorker, July 27, 1922:
        Phoebe Julia Werner was born in the town of Orangeville on March 13, 1860, the daughter of the late Philip and Philipine Dick Smith and passed away on Sunday morning July 23, 1922 at the family home at Halls Corners. In 1883 she was united in marriage to William Werner who survives, also two sons Frank and Howard, two grandchildren and two brothers and two sisters. Henry and John P. Smith, Mrs. Caroline Miller and Sarah Sergel, and a large host of relatives and friends. In early years Mrs. Werner joined the Evangelical church in which she has been an earnest worker. The community feel the loss of a good friend and neighbor as was shown by the floral and personal tributes at the service.
