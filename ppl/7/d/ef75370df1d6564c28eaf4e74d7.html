        The Attica News, 25 January 1945, p 4 - accessed online at FultonHistory.com

        Mrs. John Schlenker

        ...Mrs. Lena S. Schlenker, 76, of the Creek Rd
        died Monday night, Jan, 22, 1945, after a long
        illness. The Widow of John Schlenker who died
        several years ago, Mrs. Schlenker was born at
        Bennington on April 6, 1868, daughter of the late
        Henry and Elsie Oldschwager Sieker.

        She was a lifelong member of the Presbyterian
        Church and is survived by a daughter, Mrs. John
        D. Turrel Sr. of Attica; two sons Fred of Attica
        and George of Kansas City, Mo.; two sisters Mrs. Elsie
        Ripstein of Bennington and Mrs. Anna VanValkenburg
        of Attica; two Grandchildren, Mrs. Walter George &
        and John D. Turrel,Jr of Warsaw and one Great
        Grandchild.

        Funeral Services will be held at 2 o'clock this
        Thursday afternoon from her late home. with the Rev.
        Frank W. Terrell, pater of the Varysburg Community
        Church officiating. Burial will be in Forest
        Hill Cemetery.
