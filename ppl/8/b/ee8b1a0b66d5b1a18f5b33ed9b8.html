      • General:

        Chester E. Brown, age 87, of Orchard St., Silver Springs went to his eternal rest and reunited with his loving wife on Sunday December 11, 2011.

        He was born June 23, 1924 in Orangeville, New York a son of the late Clyde and Bessie Pfiefer Brown.

        A man of great faith he was a member of the New Beginnings Fellowship Church in Hermitage and a former member of the Oatka Baptist Church. He enjoyed deer and pheasant hunting, country music and making music whether it was singing, or playing piano, fiddle, or guitar. He was a carpet installer for Zeches Furniture in Warsaw for close to 20 years.

        On November 10, 1941 in Castile he married the love of his life, Marion L. (nee Mortimer) Brown and they enjoyed 67 years of marriage before she was called Home on November 26, 2008.

        He is survived by his children: Ruth (Keith) Hinz of Attica, David (Doris) Brown of Silver Springs, Diane (Skip) Wolcott of Perry, Ron (Pat) Brown of Hermitage; a sister-in-law: Eleanor Barnes of Easton, PA; 13 grandchildren, 31 great-grandchildren, one great-great-grandson and several nieces and nephews.

        In addition to his wife he was preceded in death by a sister and brother-in-law: Lucille and Norman Smith and a brother-in-law: Albert Barnes.

        There are no calling hours. Funeral services will be held Wednesday December 14, 2011 at Noon in the New Beginnings Fellowship Church, 4377 Route 78, Gainesville.

        Burial will be in Union Park Cemetery, Wethersfield Springs. Memorials may be made to the Silver Springs Rescue Squad.

      • General:

        Of Orchard St., Silver Springs went home to her Lord on Wednesday, Nov. 26, 2008 at the Eastside Nursing Home in Warsaw. She was 86. She was born Dec. 4, 1921 in Warsaw, a daughter of the late William Mortimer and Olive Foster Mortimer Hardenber. She was a 1940 graduate of Warsaw High School and worked for 13 years as a clerk at the former Ames Department Store in Warsaw. A woman of great faith she was a member of the New Beginnings Fellowship Church in Hermitage and was a longtime member of the Oatka Baptist Church. She enjoyed crocheting, embroidery and baking and will be remembered for her cookies, molasses in particular. Her main focus was her family---being a loving wife, proud mother and affectionate grandmother. She is survived by her husband of 67 years, Chester Brown whom she married November 10, 1941 in Castile; two daughters, Ruth (Keith) Hinz of Attica, Diane (Skip) Wolcott of Perry; two sons, David (Doris) Brown of Silver Springs, Ron (Pat) Brown of Hermitage; one sister, Eleanor (late Albert) Barnes of Easton, PA; two half-brothers, Richard Hardenber of Georgia and Russell (Delores) Hardenber of Easton, PA; 13 grandchildren; 27 great-grandchildren; one great-great-grandson; many nieces and nephews. Two sisters preceded her in death, Sydney Mortimer who died in infancey, Shirley Groff and one brother, Malling Mortimer. There was no prior visitation. A memorial service was held Nov. 30, 2008 in the New Beginnings Fellowship Church, Hermitage. W.S. Davis Funeral Home, Arcade completed the arrangements.
