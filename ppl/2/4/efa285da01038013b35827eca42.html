        Western New York, Thursday, Feb. 22, 1923:
        Martin Stortz died at his home on Center street on Tuesday morning. He was born in Germany March 18, 1847 and after coming to this country settled in Bennington. He was united in marriage to Miss Alice Smith 52 years ago. Surviving are his wife and four children, Calvin Stortz, Miss Edith Miller of Warsaw, and Mrs. Nellie Young and Mrs. Clarence Kerr of Geneva; also one sister, Mrs. Charles Webber of South Maple street, and 9 grandchildren. The funeral services were held from his late home this afternoon at 2 o'clock, Rev. W. H. Smith of Wyoming officiating. Burial in Warsaw.
