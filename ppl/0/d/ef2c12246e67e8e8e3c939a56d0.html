      • General:

        Mrs. Christina Welz, wife of Fred Welz of 52 Oatka street, Warsaw, died in Rochester on Monday, March 8th. She was born at Dutch Flats, N.Y. on March 11, 1859, the daughter of Martin and Mary Goller Schlenker and married Mr. Welz on December 2nd, 1884. Surviving besides her husband and 3 daughters, Mrs. Carl J. Werner of Buffalo, Mrs. Ray W. Johnson of Utica, Mrs. Sidney F. Hood of Oakfield, N.Y., 7 grandchildren and 4 great grandchildren. Mrs. Welz was a member of the German Evangelical church of Orangeville.
