        Of South Maple Street, widow of George J. Betz, occured the death at the Warsaw Hospital. Born in Buffalo, the daughter of Henry and Rosina Blecher Pfeifer, and she has lived in Warsaw twenty nine years. In 1876 she married Mr. Betz who is now deceased. Survived by the following children, Mrs. Mary A. Gallop of Lancaster, NY; Carl A. Betz of Warsaw, Mrs. George A. Brown of Newark, O., Mrs. Esther M. Cameron of Minneapolis, Minn., 3 grandchildren and 2 great grandchildren and the following brothers and sisters, Mrs. Anna Krauss of Warsaw; Jacob Pfeifer of Wethersfield; Mrs. Rose Pfeifer of Attica; Henry Pfeifer of Wethersfield; Rudolph Pfeifer of Wethersfield; Philip Pfeifer of LaGrange. Mrs. Betz was a member of the Methodist church.
