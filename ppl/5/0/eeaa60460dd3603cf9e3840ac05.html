        Mrs. Julia C. Fisher widow of the late John N. Fisher of Warsaw, died Friday at the home of her daughter, Mrs. Edward Holzwarth of Rochester, aged 79 years and six months. She is survived by eleven children, six sons and five daughters. The sons are George E. of Rochester; Lloyd L. and Arthur Fisher of Attica; Chester of Kirkville, John M. and Benjamin of Warsaw. The daughters are Mrs. H. P. Merle of Rochester, Mrs. Charles Boller of Sodus, Mrs. Augustus Huck of Utica, Mrs. Arthur C. Rykerd of Silver Springs and Mrs. Holzwarth of Rochester.
        A private funeral was held at the home of her daughter, Mrs. Holzwarth, 1004 Lancaster Avenue, Monday afternoon at 2 o'clock. Burial took place in Morningside cemetery,
        (Attica, NY, News, May 10, 1923)

        Note: Mrs. Arthur C. Rykerd (daughter) was Martha. They lived in Silver Springs, Maryland, and she died in 1963 in Florida.
