        Western New Yorker
        Nevinger
        Phillip Nevinger was born May 1st 1837 in Alsace, Germany, and died Saturday, May 21st 1910 in Orangeville, N.Y., aged 73 years and 20 days. At the age of 7 years he came to America with his parents and settled in Attica and later moved to Orangeville.

        In 1861 he married Christina Fisher. To this union were born 14 children, who with their mother, 4 brother, 3 sisters and 21 grandchildren survive him.

        In 1860 Mr. Nevinger united with the West Hill Evangelical church of which he was a member till his death. The funeral services were held Monday, May 23red at 1 p.m. at the house and at 2 o'clock at the Orangeville Center Presbyterian Church. Interment in the Orangeville Cemetery.

        The bereaved family wishes to express its sincere thanks and appreciation to the many friends for their assistance and sympathy in this affliction. Rev. F. M. Schwenk had charge of the funeral services.
        ----

        Philip and Christina were the parents of Charles, John, Ed, Sarah, Henry, Emma, Mary, Walter, William, Albert, Jesse, Robert, Martin and Allen.
