        The Daily News, Batavia, Saturday, September 7, 1946:
        Warsaw, Sept. 7 - Mrs. Ruth Smith Bryant, a resident of Warsaw for many years and lately of Buffalo, died in Warsaw Hospital yesterday, September 6, 2946. She was born in the town of Orangeville on October 22, 1914, a daughter of Abram and Mary Baker Smith. On October 20, 1941, she was married to Harold G. Bryant, who survives. She also is survived by her parents, Mr. and Mrs. Abram Smith of Orangeville, and 3 brothers and 3 sisters, Mrs. Arden Ewing of Espiville, Pa., Mrs. Harold Smith, Norman, Mrs. Stuart Nevinger, Laurence and Lynn Smith, all of Warsaw. She was a member of the Warsaw Evangelical Church, where services will be held at 3:00 tomorrow afternoon following prayers at the home of her parents in Orangeville at 2:15. The Rev. David B. McCleery, pastor of the church, will officiate and burial will be in Warsaw cemetery.
