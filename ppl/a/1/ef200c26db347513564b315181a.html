        Wyoming County Times, April 19, 1900:
        Henry Pfeifer died at his home in the town of Orangeville, Wednesday evening, April 11th, at the advanced age of 80 years. He was a native of Germany, and had been in this country 55 years, and a resident of Orangeville for the past 28 years. He was a man of strong character, highly respected by all who knew him. The funeral services were attended from the German church at Wethersfield Springs, Saturday April 14th at 1:00, burial in the neighboring cemetery. His wife and several sons and daughters survive him.
