        The community was sadden on Saturday morning, March 22, on hearing of the death of Philip Pfeifer at Warsaw hospital, where he ha been a patient since Thursday of last week.
        Philip Pfeifer was born October 1, 1866, at Ebenezer, N.Y, and was one of seven children born to Henry and Rosina Blecher Pfeifer. He was married Dec. 31, 1896 to Miss Flora Barber, who died March 23, 1910. On July 8, 1914, he was married to Mrs. Florence Lapham.
        Besides his widow he is survived by two sons, Roy of Jasper and Llewellyn of Perry; one step-daughter, Mrs. Carl Olsowsky; grandchildren, John Pfeifer of Jasper, Ralph Pfeifer of San Jose, Calif., Laverne Pfeifer of Collegedale, Tenn. an Miss Lorraine Pfeifer of Perry; two step grandchildren, William Olsowsky of Gainesville and Mrs. William Sager of Lamont; one sister Mrs. Anna Krauss of West Warsaw and two brothers, Jacob Pfeifer of East Aurora and his twin brother, Rudolph Pfeifer of Wethersfield Springs.
