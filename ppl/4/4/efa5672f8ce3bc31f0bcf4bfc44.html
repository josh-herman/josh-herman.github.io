        Wyoming County Times, Thursday, January 15, 1925:
        Mrs. Alice Adell Stortz widow of the late Martin Stortz died at her home on Center street January 14, 1925. Alice Smith, the youngest of 14 children, was born in Middlebury October 24, 1851, a daughter of the late Henry and Lydia Whaley Smith. The deceased is survived by four children, Calvin Stortz, Mrs Eugene Young and Mrs. Charles Miller of Warsaw and Mrs. Clarence Kerr of Geneva, 9 grandchildren and one great grandchild, also surviving is one brother, Francis Smith of Wyoming. Rev. J. G. Snyder of First Congregational church will have charge of the funeral services which will be held from the home on Friday afternoon, January 16th at two p.m. Burial will be made in Warsaw cemetery. Mrs. Stortz had been a resident of Warsaw for 22 years.

        Note: There was a request for her birthday as February 24 however obit reads October 24.
        Survived by 4 children, Edith Miller, Nellie S. Young, Genevieve Kerr and Calvin Stortz.
